alphaSounds

alphaSounds helps you learn letters and their sounds by simply typing and listening.

To get started, simply close this dialog box and press a letter on your keyboard, and you'll hear the name of the letter followed by an example of a word that starts with the sound.

At the end, just after Zed, you'll find a link to songs that feature the ABCs.
