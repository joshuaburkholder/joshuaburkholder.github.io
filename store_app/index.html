pas·tiche •

/paˈstēSH,päˈstēSH/

an artistic work in a style that imitates that of another work, artist, or period.

 

 

This gallery features cat images I have drawn, inspired by great art. Many of the cats are pastiche of recognizable art/artists (e.g., Norman Rockwell's "Triple Self-Portrat"). Others are inspired by great street art and a few are totally original.
